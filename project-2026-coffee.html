2026 Coffee
Design

CLIENT

Xillium LLC

ROLE

Packaging & Illustration

YEAR

2026

Design

  • The "Glocal" Aesthetic: A sophisticated blend of modern corporate structure and traditional Filipino folk art. The clean, matte-white pouches provide a canvas for vibrant, hand-painted illustrations.
  • Visual Hierarchy: Functional color-coding (Red, Green, Yellow) defines the roast profile, while bold sans-serif typography maintains the professional identity of the Xillium brand.
  • Storytelling Art: Each label features a central cultural vignette—Dinagyang, Kamayan, and Bayanihan—connecting the product to themes of celebration, community, and cooperation.

Purpose

  • Corporate Identity: Humanizes a tech-driven brand by aligning it with heritage and hospitality, making it memorable for clients and partners.
  • Strategic Gifting: Elevates a standard promotional item into a premium "Heritage Series" gift set, designed for high-level networking and brand activation.
  • Experiential Marketing: Uses sensory cues (specialty tasting notes and roast dates) to bridge the gap between a digital enterprise and a tangible, high-quality physical experience.
