Becker's Event
Announcement

CLIENT

Becker's Healthcare

ROLE

Graphic Design, Layout

YEAR

2026

The Challenge

Traditional healthcare event announcements often suffer from visual clutter and safe, sterile design choices. The goal was to break through the noise of corporate feeds with a structure that felt authoritative yet distinctly modern, stripped of unnecessary polish.

The Imperfection

Embracing a brutalist grid, the typography acts as the primary structural element. Instead of hiding the framework, the design exposes it. Harsh contrasts, unadorned borders, and strict monochrome application ensure the message is delivered with absolute clarity and industrial weight.
