The Objective

The goal was to produce a tiered identity solution for a tech-heavy organization. These lanyards aren't just branding; they are functional tools for internal security and role identification. By utilizing industrial patterns and a strict blue/black palette, the design bridges the gap between high-tech infrastructure and physical human assets.

Design Tiers

  • Corporate Blue: Minimalist and professional for external representation and standard staff.
  • Data Matrix: A technical, patterned design for asset control and backend operations.
  • Secure Access: High-visibility branding for rapid identification in restricted zones.
