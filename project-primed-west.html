ROLE

The Challenge

Designing the first backlit panel for a large-scale physical format. The objective was to maintain a minimal, sophisticated, and professional brand approach while ensuring high visibility in a busy exhibition setting.

The Execution

Utilizing a refined layout and stark contrasts, the panel strips away excess visual noise. The intentional restraint ensures the primary messaging remains authoritative, clear, and commands attention without relying on gimmickry.
