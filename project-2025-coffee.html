Design Elements

  • The Red: Bold, detailed clusters of ripe coffee cherries dominate the lower half, symbolizing peak flavor and readiness for harvest. This is reinforced by a vertical red divider bar.
  • The Yellow: A warm, inviting backdrop of a patterned yellow hue suggests the tropical sun and a bright, sunny acidity. Subtly outlined botanical elements add texture without clutter.
  • The Green: Distinctive green coffee leaves frame the cherry clusters, representing the natural, organic origin and fresh growth.

Purpose

The primary purpose of the design is to establish the coffee's exclusive and artisanal nature. The very small net weight of only 50g instantly signals a rare micro-lot or a special sampler pack. The specific, complex tasting notes are not merely described but presented as essential components, guiding the consumer through the curated flavor profile. The design transforms the label from a simple identification marker into a narrative of quality, care, and origin, elevating the coffee from a commodity to an exclusive experience.
